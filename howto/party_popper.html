Python Party Popper Puller

After reading about a number of wireless security devices having security issues I wanted to look at creating a workshop that introduced people to some of the concepts behind Penetration Testing. This idea was inspired after reading about the failure of Tapplock.

I decided that using an actual product was a bad idea and so I needed to create something I could use for people to do attacks on. It needed to be something that would be a little more exciting than an LED flashing on. As exciting at that can be, I wanted something that could compete with those chemistry science communicators and their explosions.

After experimenting with a couple of other ideas I came up with the idea of using a micro:bit to pull the party popper. Having it controlled over the radio module would allow for different attack vectors.
