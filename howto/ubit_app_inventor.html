Import Extensions

A couple of extensions need to be loaded in to App Inventor

  • Download the Bluetooth extension:
    http://iot.appinventor.mit.edu/assets/edu.mit.appinventor.ble.aix
  • Download the micro:bit extension
    http://iot.appinventor.mit.edu/assets/resources/com.bbc.microbit.profile.aix
